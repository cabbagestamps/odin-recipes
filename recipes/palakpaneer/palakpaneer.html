Palak Paneer

Vegetarian | <1 hour

Spicy, and so green! You may be able to get paneer (a type of cheese from India) at your local supermarket, but try finding some at a specialty shop for higher quality. The cheap stuff tends to fall apart a bit too easily.

#Ingredients

#Method

Ingredients
  • 350g spinach
  • 1 medium tomato
  • 1 onion
  • 5 large garlic cloves
  • 1 inch ginger
  • 1 green chili
  • 1 large onion
  • 1/2 cup water or as needed
  • 1/4 tsp tumeric
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/4 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 285g paneer, cubed
  • 2-3 tb heavy cream
  • Lemon juice to taste
  • 1/2 tsp crushed dried fenugreek leaves
  • Oil

Method
  1. Blanch spinach
  2. Blend (puree) together: spinach, tomato, 3 cloves garlic, ginger, green chili
  3. Heat pot on medium heat, add oil, rest of garlic, sautee until it changes colour
  4. Add onion and cook until translucent
  5. Add spinach puree, mix with 1/2 cup water
  6. Cover pot and cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally
  7. Add garam masala, tumeric, red chili, sugar, salt, cook for 1 minute
  8. Add cream and mix
  9. Stir in paneer, mix, and let simmer for 3-4 minutes
  10. Turn off heat, and add fenugreek and lemon juice
  11. Serve with rice and pappudums
