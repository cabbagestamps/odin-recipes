Chickpea Curry

Vegetarian | 1 hour
#Home

It sure is less tasty than the other curries listed here, but its much simpler, and needs no special ingredients. Can swap in sweet potato for the cauliflower

Ingredients

  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1/2 cauliflower, cut into florettes
  • 1 inch ginger, minced
  • 1/4 tsp tumeric
  • 1 tb garam masala
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 2 tins diced tomatoes
  • 1 tin coconut milk
  • 1 tin cooked chickpeas
  • Lemon juice, to taste
  • Small bag of spinach
  • Oil

Method

  1. In a pot cook onions and then add cauliflower until nearly soft
  2. Reduce heat and add spices
  3. Add tomtoes and chickpeas, cook for 15 minutes
  4. Add coconut milk and cook a further 10 minutes
  5. Take off heat and stir in lemon juice and spinach, to wilt
  6. Serve with rice and pappudums
