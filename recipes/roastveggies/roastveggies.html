Roast Veggies

This is a highly versatile recipe (and is barely a recipe at all), it can be adapted to fit whatever random veggies you happen to have in the kitchen. The staples, I believe, are potatoes and carrots, but beyond that you can add most anything you want. Just about any root vegetable will work just great, and anything else you think might roast well. Personally, I have used: potatoes, carrots, pumpkin, fennel, leek, and onion. I'm hoping to try capsicum and mushrooms as well!

  • Pro tip: Mix up the herbs and spices on each different veggie!

Ingredients

  • POTATOES
  • CARROTS
  • OIL
  • SALT
  • THATS IT
  • ADD OTHER STUFF IF YOU WANT
  • OIL AND SALT ON STUFF IN A OVEN IS TASTY

Method

  1. Preheat a fanforced oven to 200 degrees Celsius
  2. Chop them vegs up, chop smaller if you want thigns cooked faster I guess
  3. Put the salt and oil on the veggies
  4. Roast it til its cooked
  5. An hour maybe? 40 minutes? I don't know your oven
  6. Don't let it burn (alight just a little)
  7. Yum hot oil and salt on carbs yum
